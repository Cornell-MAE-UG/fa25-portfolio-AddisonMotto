## System Description

Visitors power the turbine by spinning a large external hand wheel connected to a concealed drivetrain. Rotational input passes through a custom gearbox to drive the turbine blades, creating a clear cause-and-effect learning experience.

**Key engineering challenges:**
- Designed tower and nacelle for durability under continuous public use
- Concealed all electronics and wiring within the structure
- Developed a gearbox with tolerances for smooth, reliable operation
- Implemented a clutch mechanism to limit torque and prevent injury
